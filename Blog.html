Our Honoring & Programs For 2022

All of you, our esteemed blood donors, in making regular blood donations your lifestyle, have shown the way for others to follow. You have saved countless lives, like that of Mr Algene Koh, who went on to start a family. You are the reason many individuals are alive and healthy; and many families are whole. Many beneficiaries have also decided that they too must give back to society, either through blood donation or other means. Your selfless act of giving blood, not only saves lives, but is also a multiplier-for-good in the community


Today, we also acknowledge our dedicated bloodmobile organisers - also known as BMOs. Our BMOs work tirelessly to organise blood drives, advocate giving, and recruit and retain donors in their communities. In 2015, our BMOs contributed to 29 percent of the overall blood collection. I am also happy to share that our BMOs recruited over 10,000 new donors!


This is a great occasion for us to declare our commitment to the blood programme. On behalf of the organisers, I would like to thank our event partners: Singapore Sports Hub, ST Logistics, and Hewlett Packard for their tremendous support.


Lastly, on behalf of all blood beneficiaries, and all of us from the Singapore Red Cross and Health Sciences Authority, I thank you, our champion blood donors and bloodmobile organisers for your partnership in saving lives. Indeed blood connects us all! I wish you a meaningful and enjoyable day here at the Sports Hub. Happy World Blood Donor Day!
